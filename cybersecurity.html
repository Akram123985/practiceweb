Why is Cybersecurity Important?

Cybersecurity ensures your digital safety by protecting your data, online identity, and financial information from cyber threats.

Common Cyber Threats

1. Phishing Attacks :

Fake emails or messages pretending to be from banks, social media, or tech companies asking for sensitive information.

How to Avoid It:

  • Never click on suspicious links.

  • Check the sender's email address carefully.

  • Banks and legitimate companies will never ask for your password via email.

2. Malware and Viruses :

Harmful software designed to steal your data or damage your device.

How to Avoid It:

  • Install a trusted antivirus program.

  • Avoid downloading files from unknown sources.

  • Keep your software and operating system updated.

3. Password Security & Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) :

A weak password makes it easy for hackers to access your accounts.

Best Practices :

  • Use a strong password (12+ characters, mix of letters, numbers, and symbols).

  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for added security.

  • Never reuse passwords across multiple sites.
